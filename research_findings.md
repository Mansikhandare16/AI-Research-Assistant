Introduction to AI Agents:
AI agents are software systems that use AI to pursue goals and complete tasks on behalf of users. They show reasoning, planning, and memory. AI agents are artificial intelligence that use tools to accomplish goals. AI agents have the ability to remember across tasks and changing states.

5 Key Findings:
1. AI agents don't get tired and can work 24 hours a day.
2. AI agents can provide value in situations where humans are not available or are unable to perform tasks.
3. AI agents have the ability to remember across tasks and changing states.
4. AI agents can automate tasks, analyze data, and collaborate with humans.
5. AI agents are goal-oriented, act on external environments, and operate with autonomy.
